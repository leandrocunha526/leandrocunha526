# Hi, I am Leandro Cunha :wave:

## Biography

With over a decade of experience in software development, I have been dedicated to creating and enhancing projects since 2010. My commitment to continuous learning ensures that I am always up-to-date with the latest technologies, aiming to bring added value to the products and services I work on.  

I have a strong foundation in DevOps practices and agile methodologies, which I integrate into my work to optimize development processes and project delivery.
